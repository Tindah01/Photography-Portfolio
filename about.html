About Me

Hello, I'm Kelvin Kisilu

I'm a passionate photographer and videographer with over 8 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey began when I received my first camera as a birthday gift, and since then, I've never looked back.

Specializing in wedding photography, event coverage, and portrait sessions, I strive to create images that tell stories and evoke emotions. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to deliver timeless results.

My Philosophy

I believe that photography is more than just taking pictures - it's about preserving memories, capturing emotions, and telling stories that last a lifetime. Every click of my camera is intentional, aiming to freeze moments that would otherwise be lost to time.

Equipment & Technology

I work with professional-grade equipment including Canon EOS R5 and Sony A7IV cameras, a selection of prime lenses, and advanced lighting setups. For videography, I use cinema-grade equipment to produce 4K footage with professional color grading.
